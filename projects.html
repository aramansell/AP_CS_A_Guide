RPG Project Files

Throughout the year, you build an RPG piece by piece. Each unit adds one or more files to your project. By the end, everything connects — your Dice engine rolls damage, your Combat system handles fights, your Player class stores stats, and your Dungeon generator creates adventures.

Unit 1 — Primitive Types

Dice.java

Unit 1.7Type: RPG Integration

Your dice rolling engine. Contains roll(), rollDice(), rollWithAdvantage(), isCritical(), and displayResult(). Used by every combat and random event in the game.

View Student Guide →

DiceTester.java

Unit 1.7Type: Test Harness

A main method that calls every Dice method to verify they work correctly. You run this, not Dice.java itself — Dice has no main method, it's a library.

Unit 2 — Using Objects

Player.java

Unit 2Type: RPG Integration

Character creator. Uses Scanner to read the player's name, rolls stats (STR, DEX, CON, INT) with your Dice engine, and prints a formatted character sheet. This player follows you through every unit.
