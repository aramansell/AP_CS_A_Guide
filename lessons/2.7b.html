2.7b — Character Creator: Display

RPG — Part 2 ~20 min

Part 3: printCharacterSheet() — Show Your Creation

Your character has stats but no way to display them. Build a method that prints a formatted character sheet to the console.

Requirements:

  • Method signature: public void printCharacterSheet()
  • Print the character's name, HP bar, and six stats
  • Use System.out.println() to format each line
  • The output must be clean and readable — think about spacing and alignment

Sample output:

====================
== GROG THE MIGHTY ==
====================
HP: [████████░░] 20/20

STR: 15
DEX: 10
CON: 12
INT:  8
WIS: 11
CHA:  9
====================

Socratic Checkpoint

  1. Where does printCharacterSheet() fit in the three-step pattern?
  2. The HP bar shows hp out of maxHp. What would happen if these two values get out of sync?
  3. You're printing six stats. What's repetitive about it? Can you imagine a way to clean this up? (We'll explore this with arrays in Unit 6.)

Tinker: The HP Bar

The HP bar uses a Unicode block character. Here's the formula: 

int filledBlocks = hp * 10 / maxHp;

Then, build a String with that many ■ characters followed by enough □ characters to make 10 total. Why does this formula use integer division on purpose?
