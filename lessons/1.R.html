1.R — Unit 1 Lab Day

Lab Day ~45 min

Lab days are for catching up, going deeper, and preparing for what's next. If you're behind on the RPG tasks, focus there first. If you're caught up, pick from the advanced tinker challenges below.

Priority 1: Finish Your RPG Components

  • Dice.java — roll(), rollDice(), statRoll() all working and tested
  • Character.java — generateStats() and printCharacterSheet() working
  • CharacterTester.java — generates and displays a party of 4

Priority 2: Unit 1 Bug Hunt

Your instructor left a broken program somewhere in the course files. Find it, figure out what's wrong, fix it, and explain the bug in writing.

Hint path: BugHuntUnit1.java in the demos folder.

Priority 3: Advanced Tinker Challenges

  1. Racial modifiers: Create a Race.java enum or class that applies stat modifiers (Elf: +2 DEX, -1 CON; Dwarf: +2 CON, -1 DEX). Character.java gets a race field and generateStats() applies the modifiers after rolling.
  2. HP bar visualization: Instead of 10 blocks, make the HP bar scale to the actual HP — 100 HP gets a 20-block bar, 20 HP gets a 5-block bar. Figure out the math.
  3. Party sorting: Print the party sorted by highest HP first, or sort alphabetically by name. You'll need comparison operators and careful logic (no built-in sorting yet — you're building it yourself).
  4. ASCII art title: Use System.out.println() to draw a big ASCII art title at the top of your character sheet. Look at the ASCII art resources in the docs.
